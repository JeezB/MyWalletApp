Choice of subject:

We chose to make a wallet App because we never know how much money we spend/earn.
It seem faster and easier than our banking app and it allows us to have a quick look at our expenses.

//////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////

Technologies used:

We used Ionic 3, Angular 4, Cordova and SQlite to make this app.


//////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////

In this app you can:

	- Create a new transaction with the "+" button on the top-right screen.
	- Look at the transactions on the main screen.
	- Edit those transactions by sliding them to the left on the main screen and press the blue edit button
	- Delete a transaction the same way than for editing but by pressing the delete button
	- View permanently your money balance at the bottom of the app's screen.
